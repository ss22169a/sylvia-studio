Overview

Market Differentiation in a Saturated Retail Market

BLANK SPACE entered Taiwan's competitive retail landscape targeting quality-conscious consumers who wanted something different. Our challenge was to build a brand that could cut through the noise while also scaling rapidly — from a lean e-commerce launch to five physical locations.

As Co-Founder and Marketing Lead, I owned brand positioning, customer acquisition, visual identity, and growth strategy — while managing a team and expanding operations simultaneously.

Approach

Six Strategies That Drove Growth

01

Product-Market Fit Validation

Approach

Launched with minimal investment (under $10K for e-commerce vs. $200K+ for retail) to validate demand before committing to physical expansion. The data proved strong market appetite.

02

Brand Consistency Framework

Result

Built comprehensive visual identity systems across all five locations. Testing showed consistent visual language increased brand recall by 3.2× compared to regional variations.

03

Scalable Systems Development

Approach

Created modular frameworks and operational SOPs before each store launch, enabling quality replication without the founder bottleneck — essential for rapid multi-location expansion.

04

Customer Loyalty Strategy

Approach

Implemented quarterly storytelling themes tied to brand ambassador partnerships and community engagement initiatives — building an audience, not just customers.

05

CAC Optimization

Result

Diversified marketing channels from 82% social media reliance to a balanced distribution across organic, paid, and community channels — cutting CAC by 35%.

06

Market Differentiation

Approach

Positioned BLANK SPACE against Taiwan's saturated retail market by targeting quality-conscious consumers underserved by mainstream retail — building a premium but accessible identity.
