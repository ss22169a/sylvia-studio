Building Digital Credibility for a B2B Translation Services Company

As Website Operations & Product Strategy Lead at vvis.studio, I was accountable for building and optimizing the complete digital presence for a B2B translation services company — from scratch.

The role involved developing industry-specific content across six verticals (Energy, Semiconductor, IT, Gaming, Medical, ESG), implementing SEO strategies for technical keywords, designing bilingual user experiences, and generating qualified B2B leads.

機會與策略

Five Opportunities & How I Addressed Them

01

Credibility

How might we establish trust in a competitive translation market by showcasing deep industry expertise across multiple technical sectors?

策略

Established trust through showcasing technical expertise across six industry verticals with specialized, authoritative content that demonstrated domain knowledge.

02

Discoverability

How might we improve organic search visibility for highly specific technical translation services and capture valuable inbound leads?

策略

Implemented targeted SEO strategies for technical keywords in each vertical, improving organic visibility for high-intent B2B search queries.

03

Lead Generation

How might we streamline the client acquisition journey from first visit to inquiry submission with minimal friction?

策略

Streamlined client acquisition through context-aware CTAs, gated content, newsletter segmentation, and GA4 tracking to identify high-converting pathways.

04

Multilingual Excellence

How might we maintain a bilingual website that exemplifies translation quality and reinforces our core value proposition?

策略

Designed bilingual (Chinese/English) user experiences that maintained quality and consistency across both languages without sacrificing depth or nuance.

05

Content Freshness

How might we keep content relevant across diverse industries with sustainable update strategies that maintain SEO rankings?

策略

Built sustainable content strategies and editorial calendars that enabled consistent publishing without requiring constant high-effort production.
