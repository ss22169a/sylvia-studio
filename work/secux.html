Overview

PUFido Clife Key — International Product Launch

Led international marketing for SecuX Technology's PUFido Clife Key product launch — from initial concept through to market debut at InnoVEX 2025, Taiwan's premier international tech exhibition.

The challenge was educating a market unfamiliar with PUF (Physically Unclonable Function) technology while positioning against established players like YubiKey — and doing so in a way that drove both awareness and conversion.

Opportunities & Approach

Six Opportunities & How I Addressed Them

01

Brand Portfolio Strategy

Approach

Developed unified positioning across SecuX's wallet, encryption, and authentication product lines — creating a cohesive brand narrative that emphasized security leadership.

02

Community Education

Result

Explaining unfamiliar PUF technology through visual content and simplified explainers led to a 180% increase in page time — converting skeptics into informed buyers.

03

Financial Sector Education

Approach

B2B enterprise credential-building through specialized training programs, compliance documentation, and industry-specific content targeting financial institutions.

04

InnoVEX 2025 Showcase

Achievement

Managed the product's first public demonstration at Taipei's InnoVEX 2025 — handling exhibition strategy, materials, team coordination, and press engagement.

05

Trust Signals & Conversion

Result

Improved CVR from 1.5–2% to 2.5% by diversifying trust signals — highlighting Binance and Coinbase integration, certifications, and security credentials.

06

Differentiation Strategy

Approach

Positioned PUFido Clife Key against YubiKey through superior design aesthetics, competitive pricing, and unique platform support (Binance, Coinbase) — carving a distinct market position.
