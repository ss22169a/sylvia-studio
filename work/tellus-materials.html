Overview

Sole Digital Lead for a Green Hydrogen Technology Startup

As sole Website Operations Lead at Tellus Materials, I built and managed the entire digital presence for a green hydrogen technology startup. I owned website architecture, created all technical content, developed marketing campaigns, and managed EDM distribution — across 10+ concurrent projects.

The challenge was making complex hydrogen technology understandable for non-expert B2B decision-makers, while simultaneously establishing brand authority in a competitive global market.

Key Challenges

Four Strategic Challenges

01

Technical Accessibility

Approach

Implemented a three-tier content strategy: executive summaries for decision-makers, technical overviews for evaluators, and detailed bulletins for engineers — making hydrogen tech accessible at every level.

02

Brand Authority

Approach

Established credibility competing globally through thought leadership content — technical bulletins, research highlights, and strategic partnership showcasing on the website.

03

Lead Generation

Approach

Context-aware CTAs, gated technical content, newsletter segmentation by industry, and GA4 event tracking to identify and optimize high-converting content pathways.

04

Self-Management at Scale

Approach

Systematized workflows with priority matrices, SOPs, and content calendars to manage 10+ concurrent projects solo — without sacrificing quality or deadline adherence.
