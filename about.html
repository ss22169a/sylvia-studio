About

I turn strategy into execution

Who I Am

I started in visual design — building a visual sensibility before transitioning into marketing and team leadership. That foundation shaped how I approach every problem: with both strategic clarity and aesthetic intention.

I've co-founded a retail brand, led B2B digital strategy for tech startups, managed product launches at international exhibitions, and built websites from zero. What connects all of it is a focus on cross-functional collaboration — bridging design, marketing, and product teams to deliver real results.

What Shapes My Thinking

Running marathons taught me that consistency beats intensity. Watching 200+ films gave me a deep respect for narrative and pacing. Tending 100+ plants showed me that growth needs the right conditions — not just effort.

What I've Learned

I excel at converting chaos into clarity — whether that's a product launch, a messy content strategy, or a team that needs alignment. I thrive when coordinating across functions, and I value progress from conception all the way to delivery.

What I Bring

Cross-functional collaboration expertise that spans brand building, marketing team leadership, and the product-marketing intersection. I don't just execute — I help shape the strategy behind it.
